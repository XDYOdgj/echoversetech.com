Introducing the KWANWA LED Alarm Clock

Designed for those who need a clear, easy-to-read time display, the KWANWA LED Alarm Clock features large, bold red numbers that are highly visible even in low light. Whether you're an early riser or a heavy sleeper, this clock ensures that checking the time is simple and stress-free.

The clock comes with adjustable LED brightness settings, allowing you to choose the ideal light level for your environment. It also includes an automatic night mode that activates from 8:00pm to 6:00am, reducing light output to protect your eyes and save energy during sleep hours. This makes it a great choice for anyone who values comfort and efficiency.

With a dedicated alarm button for hour and minute adjustments, setting your wake-up time has never been easier. The alarm gradually increases in volume over 60 seconds, offering a gentle and less jarring start to your day. Additionally, the included snooze function lets you rest for up to three 7-minute intervals, ensuring you stay well-rested and refreshed.

Perfect for use in the home, bedroom, or as a thoughtful gift for seniors, elderly individuals, dementia patients, or those with memory loss, the KWANWA LED Alarm Clock combines practicality with functionality. It's also battery-operated with long-lasting D-size batteries, making it portable and dependable for ongoing use.
